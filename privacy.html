This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Romero NYC Marketing Agency LLC ("Romero NYC," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you visit romero.nyc, use our web applications, Free Tools, or engage with any of our services. BY USING OUR SERVICES, YOU ACKNOWLEDGE THAT YOU HAVE READ AND UNDERSTOOD THIS POLICY. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE, PLEASE DISCONTINUE USE OF OUR SERVICES IMMEDIATELY.
Plain-English Summary
The full legal sections below are binding — this is for your convenience.
What do you collect?
Name, email, job title, company, IP address, browser/device info, cookies, and behavioral data like time-on-page and click rates.
Why do you collect it?
To run our website, deliver services, improve our tools, send marketing emails, serve ads, and reach out about our services and products.
Do you sell my data?
Not for money. We share data with service providers (HubSpot, Google, LinkedIn). This may qualify as "sharing" under CCPA — you can opt out.
What third parties get my data?
HubSpot (CRM), Google Analytics/Ads, LinkedIn Insight Tag, email delivery platforms, and our hosting provider.
What are my rights?
Access, correct, delete, or export your data. California residents have additional CCPA rights. EU/UK residents have GDPR rights. We respond within 45 days.
Do you track me with cookies?
Yes — for analytics and advertising (Google and LinkedIn pixels). You can opt out via our cookie banner or browser settings.
What about the free tools?
Free to use. By using them, you consent to us potentially reaching out about our services or products. Opt out any time.
How do I contact you?
Email kristian@romero.nyc with any privacy questions or requests.
01

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when you complete a form, subscribe to our email list, use a Free Tool, purchase a service, or communicate with us. Categories include:

  • Identifiers: First name, last name, business email address, phone number (if provided)
  • Professional information: Job title, company name, industry, company size
  • Commercial information: Service inquiries, purchase history, billing information
  • Communications: Messages, questions, and feedback you submit

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or use our tools, we and our third-party partners automatically collect:

  • Device and browser data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent per page, scroll depth, click-through rates, session duration, content engagement metrics
  • Referral data: The URL or platform that directed you to our website
  • Cookie and tracking data: As described in Section 5
  • Pixel data: Conversion events and behavioral signals captured by Google Ads and LinkedIn Insight Tag pixels

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive data from advertising platforms (Google, LinkedIn) providing audience insights, data enrichment services that supplement contact records with professional information, and analytics providers aggregating behavioral data across sessions.

1.4 Sensitive Data

Romero NYC does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as government ID numbers, health data, racial or ethnic origin, or financial account numbers. Please do not submit such information through our forms or tools.

How We Collect Your Information

2.1 Forms and Direct Submissions

Collected when you actively complete and submit a form, including contact forms, newsletter sign-ups, tool interfaces, and service inquiry forms.

2.2 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage. See Section 5 for full details.

2.3 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect behavioral data about how visitors interact with our website, including session data, page views, and engagement metrics.

2.4 Advertising Pixels

Our website deploys advertising pixels operated by Google and LinkedIn. These pixels collect visit data and may serve you retargeted advertisements on those platforms. Each platform's privacy policy applies in addition to ours.

2.5 HubSpot CRM Integration

We use HubSpot as our CRM platform. When you submit a form on our website, your information is transmitted to and stored in HubSpot. HubSpot may also track your interactions with our emails and website using its own tracking technology. See: HubSpot Privacy Policy.

2.6 Automatic Server Logs

Our web hosting infrastructure automatically records IP addresses, request timestamps, requested URLs, HTTP status codes, and referrer information for security and diagnostics.

Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction:

  • Encryption: Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Data stored in our systems and HubSpot is encrypted at rest.
  • Access controls: Personal data access is restricted to personnel who require it for their role, using role-based access controls and authentication requirements.
  • Third-party security: Key providers (HubSpot, Google Cloud infrastructure) maintain SOC 2 compliance and industry-standard security certifications.
  • Monitoring: We monitor for unauthorized access and anomalous activity.
  • Vendor assessment: We evaluate third-party service providers for security practices before sharing personal data.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities as required by applicable law (within 72 hours under GDPR where applicable).

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our Services are intended solely for adults (18+) in a B2B context. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, consistent with COPPA.

While Romero NYC does not target minors and does not create content designed to attract users under 13, we acknowledge that we cannot technically prevent minors from visiting our website and we do not have age-gating in place at this time.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take prompt steps to delete that information. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at kristian@romero.nyc.
